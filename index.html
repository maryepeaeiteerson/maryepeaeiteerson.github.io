Books and study supplies

Find where to get textbooks for your courses and study supplies.

Textbooks

Find your prescribed and recommended textbooks via the links below, then borrow, rent or buy what you need.
Semester 2 2020 textbook list (login required)

Read online

Search the Library to see if your textbook is available as an e-book. Borrowing print books via the Library is currently not possible due to coronavirus (COVID-19) restrictions.

 

Rent

Check out the The Campus Bookstore's rental service (this is an external website and has no affiliation with the RMIT Campus Store) for select textbook titles. Rentals are paid for and processed through The Campus Bookstore website.

 

Buy online or in-store

Textbooks are available from The Campus Bookstore (this is an external website and has no affiliation with the RMIT Campus Store). Purchase online and have your items posted to your home, or visit a store location.

15% discount: Log on to the textbook list link above to get your 15% discount code. The discount code applies to print textbooks only, not ebooks.

For more information or to request a particular book, contact The Campus Bookstore.

 

Course equipment

Find course equipment, lab coats, stationery and more at RMIT's Campus Store online or at City and Bundoora campuses. 
Browse course equipment at the Campus Store
